# Luxury Brand Campaign - Chanel

Featured as the face of Chanel's Spring 2024 campaign, this project embodied the timeless elegance and sophistication that the brand represents.

## Campaign Concept

The campaign focused on the modern woman - confident, elegant, and effortlessly chic. Shot in Paris, the images captured the essence of Chanel's heritage while appealing to contemporary audiences.

## Production Details

- **Location**: Paris, France
- **Duration**: 3 days
- **Photographer**: David Chen
- **Creative Director**: Marie Dubois
- **Team Size**: 15 professionals

The campaign was featured in major fashion magazines worldwide and became one of Chanel's most successful campaigns of 2024.
