# Runway Milan Fashion Week

Opening the Versace Fall/Winter 2024 show at Milan Fashion Week was a career highlight. The collection featured bold metallics and structured silhouettes that demanded confidence and precision on the runway.

## The Experience

Walking for Versace at Milan Fashion Week was a dream come true. The energy backstage was electric, and the moment I stepped onto that runway, everything felt perfect.

## Collection Highlights

The Fall/Winter 2024 collection showcased:
- Metallic fabrics with geometric patterns
- Structured blazers with dramatic shoulders
- Floor-length gowns with intricate beadwork
- The iconic Versace color palette of gold and black
